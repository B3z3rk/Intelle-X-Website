About the Project

IntelleX is a smart document search engine that combines the precision of BM25 ranking with the fluency of state-of-the-art language models.

Built with Python, Flask, and models like T5 and BART, IntelleX delivers sentence-level search results and generates clear, paraphrased responses tailored to your query.

Whether you're searching academic papers, legal texts, or business reports, IntelleX ensures you find exactly what you're looking for — fast, accurate, and secure.
